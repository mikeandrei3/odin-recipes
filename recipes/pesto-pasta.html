Pesto Pasta Recipe

Description

Pesto pasta is a simple yet flavorful dish made by tossing pasta with pesto sauce, a blend of fresh basil, garlic, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil. Itâ€™s fragrant, vibrant green, and offers a rich, herby taste.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion and Oil
  • Pesto
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boild your favorite pasta in salted water and drain.
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the basil pesto and seasonings.
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese.
