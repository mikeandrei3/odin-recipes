Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of flat pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and melted cheese, usually baked until golden and bubbling. It’s hearty, flavorful, and often enjoyed as a comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sause.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
